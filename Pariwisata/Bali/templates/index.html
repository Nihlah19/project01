INDONESIA

Indonesia is the world's largest archipelagic nation, comprising over 17,000 islands spanning across the equator between the Indian and Pacific Oceans. This diverse Southeast Asian country is home to approximately 275 million people, making it the fourth most populous nation globally. The country boasts extraordinary geographic and cultural diversity. From the lush rainforests of Sumatra and Borneo to the pristine beaches of Bali and the volcanic landscapes of Java, Indonesia's natural beauty is breathtaking. Its biodiversity is among the richest on earth, with unique species like Komodo dragons, orangutans, and an astonishing variety of marine life inhabiting its coral reefs.
